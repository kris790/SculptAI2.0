
# **SculptAI - Physique Architecture PRD (v4.0)**

## **1. Executive Summary**
SculptAI is a premium physique development platform for beginners. We focus on "Architecture before Decoration," helping users build a symmetrical V-taper frame using AI-driven ratio tracking and form mastery.

## **2. Core Innovation**
- **Physique Ratio Analytics**: Real-time tracking of shoulder-to-waist proportions (Goal: 1.5:1).
- **AI Coaching Engine**: Powered by Gemini 3 Flash for instant form feedback and physique analysis.
- **Sculpting Protocols**: Phase-based training focusing on structural width and core control.
